Our Story

Welcome to Spice Delight

Founded in 2005 by Srikanth, Spice Delight brings authentic Indian flavors to your table. Chef Chinnu, with over 25 years of culinary experience across India and abroad, envisioned a restaurant that would showcase the diverse regional cuisines of India while maintaining traditional cooking methods.

What started as a small eatery in downtown has now grown into a renowned restaurant group with multiple locations. Our commitment to quality ingredients, traditional recipes, and warm hospitality remains unchanged.

At Spice Delight, we believe food is more than sustenance - it's an experience that brings people together. Our chefs meticulously prepare each dish using authentic spices and techniques passed down through generations.
